2021-05-23

My name is Lan Xiang, I am a Ph.D. student at the National University of Singapore, under the supervision of Prof. Feng Mengling. My research focuses on designing and applying machine learning algorithms that help to solve critical healthcare problems.

Before my Ph.D. study, I graduated from the National University of Singapore with a master’s degree in electrical & computer engineering and did my research intern at IBM Research. I hold a bachelor’s degree in electronic information science & technology from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China.

Research Interests

  • Machine Learning for Health:
    • Physiological Time-series Modeling
    • Clinical Decision Making
    • Treatment Recommendation
